A 5th Hour Project by: Tristan Torres

  • All Of Us Are Dead: A Korean TV series where high school students struggle to survive in a zombie outbreak.
  • Train to Busan: A Korean movie where a dad and his daughter take a trip on a train to make it to the city of Busan, as they fight to survive a zombie outbreak on the train.
  • World War Z: A movie based off of a book where the whole world struggles to survive the zombie outbreak.
  • The Last of Us: A TV series where a fungus called Cordyceps ravages the United States. A guy and a 14-year-old girl (that is immune to the cordyceps fungus) travels the U.S. to survive the zombie outbreak.
  • Stranger Things: A TV series where a young boy vanishes, and a small town uncovers a mystery involving secret experiments, terrifying supernatural forces and one strange little girl.
  • A Quiet Place: A movie where the Abbott family struggles to survive in a post-apocalyptic world inhabited by blind extraterrestrial creatures with an acute sense of hearing.
  • A Quiet Place 2: A movie where the Abbott family now faces the terrors of the outside world as they continue their fight for survival in silence.
  • Naruto: A Japanese anime TV series based off of the manga series about the adventures of a boy with superpowers named Naruto.
  • My Hero Acedemia: A Japanese anime TV series set in a world where superpowers (called "Quirks") have become common in people. The TV show is about Izuku Midoriya, a boy who was born without a Quirk but still dreams of becoming a superhero himself.
  • Fortnite: a third-person shooter video game where up to 100 players compete to be the last person or team standing.
  • Minecraft: a video game where you are in a world made of blocks, and you can mine and craft new things. You can also build things with blocks.
  • Roblox: a video game where a global platform where millions of people gather together every day to imagine, create, and share experiences with each other in immersive, user-generated 3D worlds.
